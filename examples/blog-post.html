✨ Introducing RetroCSS v1.0

@retrodev June 15, 2024 Release

We are thrilled to announce the first official release of RetroCSS! 🎉 This framework brings the nostalgia of Win9x to your modern web projects, with a full suite of components, utilities, and theming options.

🚀 Features

  • Classic Win9x look and feel
  • Modern responsive layout
  • Dark mode & theming
  • Interactive JS components
  • Easy customization
“RetroCSS makes building nostalgic UIs fun and easy!”

🛠️ Get Started

<link rel="stylesheet" href="../dist/retro.min.css">
<script src="../dist/retro.js"></script>
